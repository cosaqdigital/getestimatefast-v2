When to request help

Signs you may need drainage solutions

  • Water sits in the yard for a long time after rain.
  • Water flows toward the home, garage, patio, or foundation edge.
  • You see erosion, washed-out soil, exposed roots, or sinking areas.
  • Downspouts discharge into problem areas or create repeated pooling.
  • Sections of the lawn stay soggy or unusable.
  • You are trying to protect landscaping, pavers, concrete, or foundation areas.

FAQ

Drainage contractor questions

When should I request drainage help?

If water repeatedly pools, flows toward the home, erodes soil, or leaves parts of the yard unusable, a drainage contractor request can help you compare next steps.

Are French drains always the answer?

No. Some properties may need grading, downspout changes, surface drains, swales, or other solutions. A local professional can evaluate the cause.

Can drainage problems affect a foundation?

Yes. Pooling water, erosion, and poor grading near the home can contribute to moisture concerns around slab or foundation areas.
