Kitchen remodeling quote FAQs

Can I request quotes for a small kitchen update?

Yes. The kitchen flow supports smaller updates like countertops, backsplash, lighting, sink upgrades, or cabinet refreshes, not just full remodels.

Do I need measurements before I start?

No. You can continue even if you only know the kitchen is small, medium, large, or open-concept.

Can photos help with pricing?

Yes. Photos are optional, but they often help professionals understand the current layout and scope more accurately.
