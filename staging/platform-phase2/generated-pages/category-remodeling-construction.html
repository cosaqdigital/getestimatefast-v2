Frequently asked questions about remodeling and construction requests

What remodeling projects can I request quotes for?

You can request quotes for kitchens, bathrooms, additions, whole-home remodeling, and other contractor-led renovation projects.

How do I know which remodeling option is right for my project?

Start with the closest remodeling option, then move into the request that feels most similar to the work you want done.

Do I have to be ready to hire someone before I request quotes?

No. You can request quotes while planning, as long as you can describe the project clearly enough for professionals to respond usefully.

Why does a remodeling request need more detail than a simple service request?

Larger renovation projects often involve multiple trades, materials, scope decisions, and timelines, so better detail leads to better first responses.
