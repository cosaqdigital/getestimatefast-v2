Frequently asked questions

Is GetEstimateFast free to use?

Yes. You can submit a request, compare options, and hear from local professionals without paying to use GetEstimateFast.

Do I have to hire someone after submitting a request?

No. There is no obligation to hire. You can review your options and choose what works best for your project.

Who will contact me after I submit?

If local availability exists, professionals who serve your area and match the service type may contact you using the contact method you selected.

Can I request more than one service?

Yes. If you need help with more than one project, you can submit a separate request for each service so the details stay clear.

Why do you ask for project details?

Project details help local professionals understand the job before they contact you, which can lead to better first responses.
