When to request help

Signs your project may need contractor coordination

  • Your project involves multiple trades or several rooms.
  • You need help comparing scope, timeline, and budget expectations.
  • You are planning a remodel, addition, or structural change.
  • You are preparing a business space for opening or renovation.
