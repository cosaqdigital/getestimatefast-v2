Quote flow concept

Smart multi-step form strategy

This is the proposed future form logic to keep lead quality high while making the process feel lighter.

1

What service do you need?

Start simple. Let users commit to the service before showing detailed questions.

2

Project details

Ask the questions that matter for that specific service, not a generic one-size-fits-all sequence.

3

Size or scope

Collect square footage, task count, room count, or affected area only when it helps estimation.

4

Timeline

Ask when the user wants the work done and use microcopy to explain why timing matters.

5

Photos

Keep photos optional and explain that they reduce follow-up and improve quote accuracy.

6

Location

Collect the city or ZIP before the final contact step to support routing and local matching.

7

Contact information

Ask for name, phone, and email only after value and trust are well established.
