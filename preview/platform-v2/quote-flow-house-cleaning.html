Fast flow example

House Cleaning quote flow

This flow is built for simpler services where speed matters more and users should not face the same level of friction as a remodeling request.

Progress bar concept

Step 1 of 5 About 60 seconds No obligation

1. Cleaning type

House cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in cleaning, move-out cleaning, office cleaning, or Airbnb cleaning.

2. Home size or task count

Bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage range, or job size depending on the selected cleaning service.

3. Timeline and frequency

One-time, weekly, biweekly, monthly, or urgent. This helps route the lead correctly without asking too much.

4. Location

Ask for city and ZIP so the request can be matched locally before asking for full contact details.

5. Contact information

Keep this final step short, clear, and paired with trust copy about how information is used.

Key difference from the complex flow

Simple services should move faster, ask fewer technical questions, and avoid making the request feel like work.
