FAQ

Questions users need answered before they submit

These answers should reduce fear about spam, explain why details matter, and support SEO.

Is GetEstimateFast free to use?

Yes. Users can request quotes and compare local professionals without paying to submit a request.

Why do you ask detailed project questions?

Detailed requests help professionals understand the work, estimate more accurately, and reduce avoidable follow-up calls.

Will my information be shared widely?

The recommended platform message is that information is only shared with relevant local professionals for the selected request.

Can I request help for residential and commercial projects?

Yes. The future platform should support homeowners, landlords, and businesses with service-specific request flows.
