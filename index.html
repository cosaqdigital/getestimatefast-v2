FAQ

Common questions before you start

Short answers to help you understand what happens after you submit a request.

Is GetEstimateFast free to use?

Yes. You can describe your project, compare options, and request help from local professionals without paying to use GetEstimateFast.

What happens after I submit a request?

Your request is reviewed by service type, location, and timing so relevant local professionals can understand the project before they contact you.

Will my information be sent to a lot of companies?

No. Your request is meant for only relevant local professionals for that service and area, not a broad list of unrelated companies.

Why do you ask for project details?

Helpful project details give local professionals more context before they contact you, which can lead to better first responses and fewer repetitive follow-up questions.

Can I request more than one type of service?

Yes. If you need help with more than one project, you can submit another request so each service gets the right details.
